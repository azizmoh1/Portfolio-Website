I'm a junior mechanical engineering student with a hands-on approach to design and analysis. My work spans parametric CAD in SolidWorks and Creo, finite element analysis in ANSYS, and computational modeling in MATLAB and Python. I care about the details — tolerances, material selection, manufacturing constraints — the things that separate a sketch from something that actually works.

Recent projects include designing a decorative cantilever traffic signal pole for Chicago intersections, validated through ANSYS static structural FEA with a minimum safety factor of 4.6, and a computational truss optimization study in MATLAB. I'm also building a predictive analytics pipeline for Formula 1 race dynamics using Python and machine learning.

I'm currently preparing for the FE exam and actively looking for internship opportunities where I can contribute to real engineering problems. If you want to talk design, analysis, or anything engineering — reach out.
