I'm a junior mechanical engineering student with a hands-on approach to problem solving. Most of my time goes into designing parts in SolidWorks or Creo, building physical prototypes, and analyzing systems with MATLAB and Python. I care about the details — tolerances, material choices, manufacturing constraints — the things that make an idea actually work in the real world.

Outside of pure engineering, I've developed a strong interest in data-driven analysis. My Formula 1 analytics project sits at the intersection of those two worlds: using telemetry data and machine learning to understand and predict race dynamics.

I'm currently working toward my FE exam and building up my project portfolio. If you're interested in collaborating or just want to talk engineering, feel free to reach out.
