Truss Design & Computational Analysis Competition

MATLAB | Statics | Optimization

Project Overview

Developed a computational solver and visualization workflow to evaluate truss internal forces and optimize structure efficiency for competition scoring.

Problem Statement

Design a truss that maximizes load capacity while minimizing total member length, under strict statics and failure constraints.

Design Process

  • Built force resolution logic for all members in one analysis loop.
  • Defined boundary conditions and support constraints for stability.
  • Iteratively refined node/member layout to reduce redundancy and improve central load paths.

Key Results

Lessons Learned

Systematic iteration and data-driven geometry updates produced better structural performance than ad-hoc adjustments, especially at high-stress center members.
